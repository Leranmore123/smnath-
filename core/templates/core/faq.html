Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about wallet top-ups, printing durations, and refund processing.

How long does it take for manual government services to process?
Manual applications (such as Senior Citizen Card, Gruha Lakshmi Sanction Order, RTC land documents, tailoring certificate, etc.) are processed by our admin agents within 12 to 24 hours. Once completed, a high-quality PDF will be uploaded directly to your service logs, and you will be able to download it with a single click.
How do instant print services work?
Instant print services (e.g. PAN card downloads, Voter ID retrieval, Ration card printing format) are fully automated. Our systems instantly query corresponding government databases and format the returned details into a high-quality print card PDF. This processes in real-time (under 5 seconds) and immediately deducts the cost from your wallet balance.
My recharge request is still pending. What should I do?
All credit request balances via UPI scan require manual validation of the UTR transaction reference ID. Recharges are normally verified and approved by admins within 5 to 10 minutes (available between 8:00 AM and 10:00 PM). If your recharge has been pending for over 30 minutes, please click on the Chat Support button below and share your registered email and the payment receipt.
What happens if my application is rejected?
If our admin agents cannot process your request (e.g. details like EPIC number or Aadhaar entered were invalid, or server is down), the application will be marked as REJECTED. The system will automatically refund the full debit amount back to your wallet balance instantly. You will see the refund reflected immediately in your dashboard.
What is the minimum wallet recharge limit?
To prevent spam transactions, the minimum amount you can add to your wallet at any single time is ₹200. There is no maximum limit, and the balance does not have any expiration date.
